World News Headlines - June 17, 2026
===================================

1. Domestic & Security News (U.S.)
---------------------------------
* Foiled White House Plot: The FBI has successfully disrupted a domestic terrorist plot targeting the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) event held at the White House last weekend. The planned attack reportedly involved utilizing explosive-laden drones to trigger a mass evacuation, which would be followed by sniper fire. Five individuals have been charged across multiple states, and investigators have identified a broader network of 23 individuals connected to the plot.
* Georgia Primary Elections: Rick Jackson secured the Republican nomination for Governor of Georgia. Additionally, Congressman Mike Collins won his primary and is set to face incumbent Democratic Senator Jon Ossoff in the upcoming November general election.
